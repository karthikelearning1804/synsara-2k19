iNovate

The iNovate Business Plan competition is part of the SynSara’2k19 Annual Techfest and simulates an angel investor or venture capital pitch. Teams will present their business proposals to a panel of (business professionals and investors/judges) in a presentation format, with the strongest ideas and teams moving forward to compete for substantial cash prizes that could be used to further the commercialization of their inventions.

Business Plan Competition students learn key business concepts and skills while developing a blueprint strategy to launch a new product or service. This competition helps young people build skills, unlock creativity, and discover pathways to opportunities in small and large businesses. This unique opportunity helps students get excited about the world of business and entrepreneurship and empowers them to use entrepreneurial thinking to create value, in their own companies or working for someone else.

Rounds:

  1. Qualifying Round -Qualifying Round - Submissions consist of a presentation which has to be sent to

    inovate.synsara@gmail.com

    in the following format:
    • Subject: iNovate (Your Team_Name)
    • Body: Your Business plan as in the given presentation template.
    • Presentation Template: Click Here

      • Note: The teams qualified in the qualifying round will appear for the final round.
  2. Final Round - Powerpoint Presentation and Evaluation

Last date for registering & submission: 15th Sept 2019

Rules

  1. A team can consist of maximum of 4 members.
  2. Presentations are limited to 10-15 minutes and will be followed by 5 minutes of Q&A by the judging panel.
  3. The selected business plans will be intimated through e-mail.

    4. Note: Further details will be given in the same.So, kindly register to participate.

  4. Though teams can choose their presenting person or persons, each student member should be present at the competition and be prepared to support their business plan.
  5. The selected teams must bring a Hard copy and the Soft copy of their presentations. Please bring your own pendrives for the Final Round.
  6. The Final Presentation should not exceed more than 30 slides.
  7. The Jury decision will be final and binding.
